Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

What this is

FYND TODAY is a sound-based recognition app. You search for or listen to a track, and the app gives you a few words to tap based on what stands out to you in the moment. This page explains what we collect when you use it, and why.

What we collect

If you give us your email, we keep it so we can send you what you've signed up for — recaps, recognition forms, and updates from FYND TODAY. We use Brevo to send these emails.

When you search for or listen to a track, we log basic activity tied to that session — the track, the words you tapped, and the position they map to. We use Airtable to store this. We use it to build your recognition forms and, if you bring the same track back, to notice the pattern.

We don't run ad trackers, third-party analytics scripts, or sell any data to anyone. We don't track your location.

YouTube

Track search and playback are powered by YouTube. When you search for a track, that search is sent to YouTube's API to find it. When you listen, the track plays through an embedded YouTube player. YouTube's own privacy practices apply to that part of the experience — you can read those at policies.google.com/privacy.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your information to anyone.
  • We don't show you ads.
  • We don't share what you tap or listen to with anyone outside FYND TODAY.

Your control over your data

You can ask us to delete your email and any activity tied to it at any time by reaching out at hello@fyndtoday.com. We'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

If this changes, we'll update this page. The date at the top will reflect the most recent update.

Questions

Reach out any time at hello@fyndtoday.com.
